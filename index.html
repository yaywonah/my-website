I'm Ye-Won!

As a front end developer I prioritize user experience, especially as the digital landscape continues to grow.

Coding is my way of making sense of all that information and conveying it to others. I like to create digital spaces with accessibility in mind, so that everyone can have a meaningful online experience. Accessbility is crucial whether it's coding for fun, like creating fan shrines for Korean dramas, or for education, like compiling poems in a database to keep track of a poet's long-standing career. The Internet makes it possible to share that information, and I enjoy finding the best way to both present that information and connect with my audience. When I'm not coding, I'm usually reading fantasy novels or crocheting animals to add to my amigurimi collection.

Can't wait to work with you!
